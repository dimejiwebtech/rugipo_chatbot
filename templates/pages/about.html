About RUGIPO AI Chatbot

Project Overview

This AI-powered chatbot is designed to improve student support services at Rufus Giwa Polytechnic, with a special focus on the Faculty of Engineering. The system provides instant answers to student queries, reducing wait times and improving overall student experience.

Objectives

  • • Provide 24/7 student support services without human limitations
  • • Answer frequently asked questions about registration, exams, and fees
  • • Guide students through administrative processes step-by-step
  • • Offer specialized support for Faculty of Engineering students

Technology

Built with Django framework, OpenAI's advanced language models, and modern web technologies to deliver fast, accurate, and helpful responses. The system combines artificial intelligence with a curated knowledge base to provide the best possible support experience.
