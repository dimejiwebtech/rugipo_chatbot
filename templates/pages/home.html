Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers about the RUGIPO Student Support Chatbot and how it can help you.

What kind of questions can I ask the chatbot?

You can ask about course registration, school fees, hostel accommodation, departmental requirements, campus facilities, academic calendar, exam schedules, and general information about Rufus Giwa Polytechnic. The chatbot is designed to help with any student-related queries.

Is the chatbot available 24/7?

Yes! The RUGIPO Student Support Chatbot is available round the clock to assist you. Whether it's early morning or late at night, you can get instant answers to your questions anytime you need help.

How accurate is the information provided?

The chatbot is trained with official RUGIPO information and is regularly updated to ensure accuracy. However, for critical decisions or official documentation, we recommend verifying with the relevant school office or department.

Can the chatbot help with personal student issues?

The chatbot provides general guidance and information about school policies and procedures. For personal issues requiring individual attention, such as grade disputes or disciplinary matters, you'll need to contact the appropriate department or faculty office directly.
